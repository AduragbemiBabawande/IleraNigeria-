About Us

IleraNigeria

IleraNigeria is a non-profit organisation committed to impacting local communities in Nigeria in the area of healthcare and development through research, advocacy, health promotion and health care service delivery.

Our vision is to build healthy local communities by promoting access to health care services and healthy life practices.

We are raising a community of members and volunteers who are passionate and deliberate about access to healthcare in local communities and will become key influencers in achieving our vision.

Our Work

Thumbs & Bubbles

Through the Thumbs & Bubbles program, Ilera Nigeria teaches young elementary school pupils about water, hand hygiene and safe hand practices in a bid to avoid contamination with germs and reduce the outbreak of diseases like cholera. Thumbs & Bubbles has been executed in not less than 10 public schools in different communities in Nigeria impacting over 2000 students. It is a week-long activity in celebration of the Global Hand Washing Day which takes place on the 15th October every year. This project not only teaches the pupils how to properly wash their hands, but also provides them with hygiene kits and academic materials.

Cancer, Cares & Cure

This project is dedicated to raising awareness on common cancer cases like breast cancer, cervical cancer, colon & prostate cancer in rural communities. This project takes different forms annually. Our activities are carried out through awareness walks, seminars, social media campaigns, and outreaches done in partnership with primary health facilities, and other organisations.

All Positive

This project was launched in 2018. It is aimed at educating the public on Sexually Transmitted Infections with a special focus on HIV AIDS. IleraNigeria carries out assessment of HIV/AIDS using the Rapid Diagnostic Test kits, creates awareness on the current trends and research on HIV/AIDS with the aim of reducing/eliminating the stigmatization of people living with HIV AIDS (PLWHA), and equally connect the PLWHA to medical centers where they can get Anti-retroviral drugs.

16 in 18 communities

Under this programme are other initiatives of the IleraNigeria carried out in local communities including but not limited to basic medical assessment and screening, counselling and medical consultations, and referrals to the nearest health facility. At the center of these activities is education on hygienic lifestyle and preventive measures against any disease condition.
