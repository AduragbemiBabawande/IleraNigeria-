Achievements

2017

16 IN 18 COMMUNITIES

Through our 16 in 18 Communities program, IleraNigeria reached out to 200 artisans (50 Barbers, 100 Automobile Repairers and 50 Market Stall Owners) on occupational health hazards and prevention.

Distribution of Insecticide Treated Nets (ITN's) and free drugs to pregnant women in Laisiese and Idita communities in Ile-Ife, Osun state in partnership with the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) (a Faith based organisation).

CANCER CARES AND CURES

Through our “Cancer, cares and cure” program, we educated about 100 young girls in Vickayor secondary school in Ile-Ife town, Osun state on breast self-examination for breast cancer prevention.

2018

16 IN 18 COMMUNITIES

We had through our 16 in 18 communities project, a “Farmers’ day”, where we visited local farmers on awareness of occupational hazards and prevention.This project impacted 100 farmers

CANCER CARES AND CURES

With over 100 Volunteers, we impacted over 5,000 community members in Ile- Ife, Moro and Shekona town in Osun state through our Cancer, cares and cure program, a pink October walk towards the prevention of breast cancer and practices of breast self-examination in partnership with the Red cross society, junior chamber (JCI), Obafemi Awolowo university medical student asspciation (IFUMSA), AIESEC

Thumbs and Bubbles

Through our Thumbs and bubbles Project we reached out to 10 primary school students to teach them on hand hygiene and distributed WASH MATERIALS

All Positive

Through the “All positive” program, we impacted over 5000 community members in Ile-Ife town and Tonkere Village in Osun State, creating an awareness on HIV/AIDS and a support group for people living with HIV/AIDS (PLWHA).

We also educated about 50 young girls (aged 16-20years) on sexual transmitted diseases and risky sexual behaviours in a bid to curb teenage pregnancy and unsafe abortion

2019

16 IN 18 COMMUNITIES

Through our 16 in 18 communities project, we conducted basic medical assessment for Sabo community members, medical consultation and distributed free drugs. This project impacted 30 number of persons.

All Positive

Through our All positive program, we had the “Red umbrella walk”, an HIV/AIDS awareness in Ilesha town, had HIV/AIDS screening done for about 100 community members, and distributed condoms for sexual protection

2020

16 IN 18 COMMUNITIES

Through our 16 in 18 communities program, we had an outreach to Abagboro village in Osun state, where basic medical assessment was conducted, free drugs and ITN’s were distributed. This project impacted 1000 villagers

Thumbs and Bubbles

Through our Thumbs and bubbles program, “100days awareness on COVID-19”, we engaged the media on preventive measures against COVID-19, interviewed survivors via webinars, and distributed personal protective equipment and WASH materials to the vulnerable populations which include but not limited to the front line health care workers in partnership with Global Health and Adolescent Development Initiative (GADHI).

2021

Thumbs and bubbles

Through our Thumbs and bubbles Project we reached out to a primary school students in Kosere community to teach them on hand hygiene and distributed WASH MATERIALS

All Positive

We partnered with some1Nigeria to reach out to 200 secondary school students in discussing Gender based violence, Sexual health and reproductive health rights

We also had a webinar on HIV pre-exposure and post exposure prophylaxis, thereby increasing awareness and a medical outreach in Ile-Ife community in partnership with Nigerian youth service corps Osun- State chapter, it’s a day free screening for the community as well as counselling and education in commemoration of the WORLD AIDS DAY 2021
