About Us

IleraNigeria

IleraNigeria is a non-profit organisation committed to impacting local communities in Nigeria in the area of healthcare and development through research, advocacy, health promotion and health care service delivery.

Our vision is to build healthy local communities by promoting access to health care services and healthy life practices.

We are raising a community of members and volunteers who are passionate and deliberate about access to healthcare in local communities and will become key influencers in achieving our vision. We are committed to continuous collaboration and partnership with organisations, government, as well as others who are pivotal in the achievement of our vision. We are passionate about community-based outreaches and research activities targeted at different groups of people who share common interest either by vocation, geographic location, or belief.

We achieve our vision through our set of structured of programs directed at a specific group of people per time, including but not limited to the following: 'Thumbs & Bumbles', 'Cancer, Cares & Cures', 'All Positive' and '16 in 18 communities' are some of the defined programs
